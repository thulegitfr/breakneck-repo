About breakneck

What does the company do?

breakneck is a streetwear clothing brand made for Gen Z. We sell everyday clothes like jeans, jackets, t-shirts, hoodies, and other basics. Our goal is to make clothes that are comfortable, trendy, and affordable for students and young adults.

Who is the brand for?

This brand is for people who like casual street style and want clothes they can wear daily. breakneck is for anyone who wants to look good without spending too much money. We focus on comfort, confidence, and simplicity.

Why buy from breakneck?

  • Affordable prices for students and young adults
  • Simple streetwear styles that are easy to wear
  • Good quality materials that last
  • Clothes that follow current trends
  • A brand that feels casual and relatable
