Branding Ideas

What does the company do?

Stitch N Snitch is a streetwear clothing brand made for Gen Z. We sell everyday clothes like jeans, jackets, t-shirts, hoodies, and other basics. Our goal is to make clothes that are comfortable, trendy, and affordable for students and young adults.

Who is the brand for?

This brand is for people who like casual street style and want clothes they can wear daily. Stitch N Snitch is for anyone who wants to look good without spending too much money. We focus on comfort, confidence, and simplicity.

Why would someone buy from Stitch N Snitch?

  • Affordable prices for students and young adults
  • Simple streetwear styles that are easy to wear
  • Good quality materials that last
  • Clothes that follow current trends
  • A brand that feels casual and relatable

Brand Components

Fonts

Primary Font: Open Sans

Open Sans is the main font used on the Stitch N Snitch website. It is easy to read and works well on both desktop and mobile screens. The font feels modern and casual, which matches the brand.

Where it is used:

  • Body text
  • Navigation
  • Buttons
  • Product descriptions

Font Weights:

  • Regular (400) for normal text
  • Bold (700) for headings and important text

Colour Palette

Main Colours

  • Red (#FF0000): Used to grab attention and show energy
  • Black (#000000): Makes the design feel bold and clean
  • White (#FFFFFF): Keeps the layout simple and readable

Secondary Colours

  • Dusty Rose (#AA8686): Used for warm background areas
  • Dark Red (#8B0000): Used for hover states and highlights
  • Grey (#808080): Used for borders and small details

These colours were chosen to match streetwear culture. Black and white keep things simple, while red adds energy. The extra colours help the site feel balanced and not boring.

Imagery and Art Direction

  • Photos taken in urban locations like streets and skateparks
  • Natural lighting instead of studio lighting
  • Real people with different body types
  • Simple and bold layouts
  • Minimal editing to keep images realistic
