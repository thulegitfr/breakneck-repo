Branding Ideas

What does the company do?

Y2Klothing is a streetwear clothing brand made for Gen Z. We sell everyday clothes like jeans, jackets, t-shirts, hoodies, and other basics. Our goal is to make clothes that are comfortable, trendy, and affordable for students and young adults.

Who is the brand for?

This brand is for people who like casual street style and want clothes they can wear daily. Y2Klothing is for anyone who wants to look good without spending too much money. We focus on comfort, confidence, and simplicity.

Why would someone buy from Y2Klothing?

  • Affordable prices for students and young adults
  • Simple streetwear styles that are easy to wear
  • Good quality materials that last
  • Clothes that follow current trends
  • A brand that feels casual and relatable

Brand Components

Fonts

Primary Font: Helvetica

Helvetica is the main font used on the Y2Klothing website. It is easy to read and works well on both desktop and mobile screens. The font feels modern and casual, which matches the brand.

Where it is used:

  • Body text
  • Navigation
  • Buttons
  • Product descriptions

Font Weights:

  • Regular (400) for normal text
  • Bold (700) for headings and important text
